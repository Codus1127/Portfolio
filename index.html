About Me

"I'm not an inspirational quote kind of person." - Chrissy Teigen

I am an Oregonian who moved to Utah in 2010. I attended Brigham Young University and in 2017 graduated with a B.S. in Exercise Science. I'm married to the woman of my dreams and enjoy hiking, skiing, and just about any sport out there.

My love for code started in 2014 when one of my classes introduced me to HTML/CSS, SQL, and Excel VBA. Since then I pursued an education in Javascript, Node, and PostgreSQL and have loved every minute of it. There is nothing quite like seeing your creation and ideas come to life with each new line.

A Current Project

TicketsFor2

A user can view, match, and chat with other users as well as create and manage their own account. Login/Registration flow is facilitated through Facebook and Firebase.
Project was jumpstarted using Expo and later converted to pure React Native. Push Notifications are being managed through FCM. IAP is currently being added. The app is in Beta on Google Play and Testflight for iOS. Team collaboration and version control was accomplished using GitLab.

React Native | React Navigation | Expo | NodeJS | Express | Socket.io | Massive | Heroku | PostgreSQL | Firebase | Amazon S3

My First Project

And One Analytics

A user friendly, full-stack application that serves high-school and prep league basketball coaches an easy to use and persistent stat tracking platform. Users undergo a secure registration process and experience a private dashboard that allows them to add teams, players, and stats. Data visualization is done utilizing Chart.js to allow for easy trend identification.

React | Redux | HTML5 | CSS3 | NodeJS | Axios | Express | Express Session | Massive | PostgreSQL | Bcrypt | Chart.js
